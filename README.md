# WEB103 Prework - Mukbang World

Submitted by: Daniel Chen

About this web app: Mukbang World is a web app designed to help users discover and explore popular Mukbang YouTubers. Mukbang, a trend originating from South Korea, features content creators eating large quantities of food while interacting with their audience. With Mukbang World, users can browse a curated list of Mukbang YouTubers, view detailed profiles, and find new creators to follow. Whether you're a fan of ASMR eating sounds, vibrant personalities, or unique food challenges, Mukbang World offers a gateway to the most entertaining Mukbang channels on YouTube.

Time spent: 3 hours
